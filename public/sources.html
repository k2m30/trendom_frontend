How it works

Our sources

We do a lot of emailing ourselves. In the last two years we have studied different techniques of personal data mining, ranging from how to optimise a simple Google search, to Facebook API access and finally to quite sophisticated approaches involving social networking and manual research.

We have collected a huge amount of public data from more than 200 social networks and other open sources. It requires a lot of time and effort to maintain this database and keep it up to date, but we do it, and as a result, it is growing constantly.
